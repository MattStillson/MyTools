​Beethoven

After centuries of silence one of the greatest artists of all time is emerging with a new album and supporting tour. Beethoven's new work is a fresh mixture of classical symphonies with bluegrass and neo-Latin influences. While the new compositions are first-rate, the stage show is reminiscent of early Beatles with industrial sensibilities. This show is a must-see!
